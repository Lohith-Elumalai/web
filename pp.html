Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details, when you place an order or sign up for our services. We also collect non-personal information, such as browsing data, cookies, and usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used to process orders, improve user experience, and communicate with you about products, services, and promotions. We may also use your information for analytics and research purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in running our website or processing orders, but only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. These cookies help us remember your preferences, improve site functionality, and track usage statistics. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be noted at the top.
