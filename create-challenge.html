Challenge Guidelines

🎯

Be Specific

Define clear criteria for your challenge to help participants understand what you're looking for.

💡

Be Creative

Think outside the box and create challenges that inspire unique and innovative outfit combinations.

🤝

Be Inclusive

Create challenges that allow participation across different styles, genders, and fashion preferences.

📅

Set Reasonable Timeframes

Give participants enough time to create and submit their entries while keeping the challenge exciting.
