Career Opportunities

Sales Representative

We are looking for dynamic and motivated individuals to join our sales team. As a Sales Representative, you will be responsible for engaging with customers and driving sales for our products. Prior experience in sales is preferred.

Manager

We are seeking a highly organized and experienced individual to manage our store operations. The Manager will oversee day-to-day operations, supervise staff, and ensure that the store is running efficiently. Leadership skills and experience in retail management are essential.

Receptionist

The Receptionist will be the first point of contact for visitors and customers. Strong communication and organizational skills are necessary. Responsibilities include answering phones, greeting customers, and assisting with various administrative tasks.

Sales Executive

We are hiring a Sales Executive to handle customer inquiries, promote our products, and drive sales growth. Strong interpersonal and communication skills are a must. Experience in sales is an advantage, but not required.
